Our Story

“Ultra Worx Ltd. was born from a desire to be a working-at-height solutions group that puts safety above everything else. Our founders—Evelake Halcyon, Tony Lam, and Michael Carver—noticed a troubling gap in the rope access industry. Despite its importance, safety, inspection, and proper usage were often overlooked, leading to stalled risk assessments and unchecked unknowns. Determined to change this, we built our company on the foundation of industry expertise, innovation, and an uncompromising commitment to safety. Today, our proven track record on some of the largest construction sites in the country speaks to our relentless pursuit of excellence.”

Our Approach

We are a technical solutions group committed to overcoming access challenges in even the most complex environments. Our IRATA-certified technicians use advanced techniques to safely reach and work in difficult-to-access areas, ensuring that projects are completed efficiently and to the highest standards.

Proven Track Record

With years of experience delivering successful projects for clients in construction, industrial maintenance, and facility management sectors, Ultra Worx continues to push the boundaries of what's possible at height.

Our Commitment to Safety and Quality

Every project begins with a rigorous review by our operations team, followed by detailed assessments by our health and safety department. This structured process ensures that every risk is managed and every task meets the highest safety standards. Our quality director plays a pivotal role in upskilling our team and evaluating every completed job, reinforcing our commitment to both quality and safety. We follow ISO9001 and ISO45001 guidelines and are on track to obtain full certification later this year.

Tailored Solutions for Unique Challenges

We understand that every client and project is unique. Whether you require rope access for construction, industrial maintenance, or facility management, we tailor our services to address your specific needs—from the initial proposal through to final confirmation and seamless execution.

Our Vision

Our ambition is to set the industry benchmark for rope access safety in the UK. By consistently delivering safe, efficient, and reliable solutions, we aim to not only elevate our own standards but also inspire the entire industry. Our goal is clear: to make the UK the safest rope access country in the world. Thank you for considering Ultra Worx Ltd. as your trusted partner for working-at-height solutions. We look forward to delivering exceptional service that ensures safety, quality, and peace of mind every step of the way.
