Our Story

“Spider Worx Ltd. was built on a foundation of industry expertise, innovation, and a commitment to excellence in rope access and height safety solutions. With extensive experience in delivering high-risk, high-access projects, we understand the challenges our clients face and provide tailored solutions that prioritise safety, efficiency, and compliance.”

“Our founders, Evelake Halcyon, Tony Lam and Michael Carver, have worked in the rope access industry for over 15 years combined, managing small to large-scale projects across the UK.”

Our Approach

We are a technical solutions group committed to overcoming access challenges in even the most complex environments. Our IRATA-certified technicians use advanced techniques to safely reach and work in difficult-to-access areas, ensuring that projects are completed efficiently and to the highest standards.

Proven Track Record

With years of experience delivering successful projects for clients in construction, industrial maintenance, and facility management sectors, Ultra Worx continues to push the boundaries of what's possible at height.
